Bio

Roxy Kotzin is a dentist and soon to be full stack developer living in Bend, Oregon. She decided on a career in dentistry at a young age as a way to combine art, science, technology, and as a way to give back to her community. She currently practices at the #1 rated practice in the city.
She received her doctorate in 2011 from Oregon Health and Science University School of Dentistry. She’s passionate about lifelong learning and always working to improve her skill set. She maintains active memberships in numerous professional organizations, both local and national.
Her free time these days is mostly spent coding. Otherwise you’ll find her at home or outdoors with her husband, their toddler, dog and cat, reading, rock climbing, relaxing, or planning her family’s next travel adventure.
